Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026 · SurgeonAccess Inc dba SurgeonValue

SurgeonAccess Inc ("SurgeonValue", "we", "us") operates surgeonvalue.com. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, practice details) provided during signup
  • Clinical encounter data you submit for billing analysis
  • Usage data (features accessed, session duration) for product improvement
  • Payment information processed by Stripe (we do not store card numbers)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide revenue intelligence and billing analysis services
  • To generate prior authorization requests and clinical documentation
  • To send product updates and support communications
  • To improve AI model accuracy (de-identified data only)

HIPAA Compliance

SurgeonValue operates as a Business Associate under HIPAA. We maintain a signed BAA with customers who submit Protected Health Information (PHI). Clinical data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell PHI.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with: (a) service providers necessary to operate the platform (Supabase, Anthropic, Stripe, Vercel), each bound by confidentiality agreements; (b) as required by law.

Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 7 years for HIPAA compliance. You may request deletion of non-PHI data by contacting us.

Security

We use AES-256 encryption, SOC 2-compliant infrastructure, and role-based access controls. All data is stored in US-based data centers.
