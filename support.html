Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Email billing@surgeonvalue.com with your account email. We cancel within 1 business day with no penalty.

What is the $20/encounter fee?

After the AI identifies and confirms a billable code or modifier, a $20 per-encounter fee applies to Core and Pro plans. You only pay when revenue is recovered.

Is my clinical data secure?

Yes. SurgeonValue is HIPAA-compliant. All PHI is encrypted in transit and at rest. We sign a BAA with all customers who submit clinical data.

What EMR systems do you support?

Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, and Modernizing Medicine. Additional integrations available on request.
